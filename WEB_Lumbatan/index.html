About Me

An enthusiastic and aspiring Digital Artist and Animator, a 2nd year art student at the University of Northwestern Mindanao major in BS Animation and Multimedia Arts.

I am currently one of the Digital Artist and Cartoonist in The Insights Publication, creating artworks for the school under the publication. As a digital artist, I seek to produce quality artworks that conveys emotion and deep messages to my audience, and I would like to extend my expertise to everyone who wants my help. I am willing to take any project to the extent of my capabilities, and submit it in a punctual manner.

I aspire to create more artworks and become an animator in the near future!
